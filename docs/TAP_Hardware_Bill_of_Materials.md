# TAP Hardware Bill of Materials (BOM) & Component Logs
## Component Specifications, Markings, and Functions in TAP Hardware Builds

This document lists all capacitor, diode, and semiconductor component types used across the **TAP Macroscopic Qubit Sandwich**, the **8-Channel Tetrahedral Bridge**, and the **Graphene Exfoliation Transducer Driver**. It maps each part to its function and matching photographic log in the repository.

---
