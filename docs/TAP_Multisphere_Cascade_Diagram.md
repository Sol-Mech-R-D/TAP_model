as the universe expands. The cosmological cascade sweep
shows the sequence:

## Why this matters (v5.3 finding)

The v5.2 framing was: the next Exhale's biological
templates are a clean replay of the previous cycle's
biological data, preserved through the Inhale.

The v5.3 finding is: **the reset is NOT clean**. Some
residue survives:
  - **Soot/PAHs** (refractory organics from thermal
    decomposition)
  - **Magnetite** (refractory iron oxide)
  - **L-D alternating peptides** (chiral antagonists from
    racemization)
  - **Acoustic/electrical dampeners** (amorphous glass)

These **anti-templates** bias the next cycle's templates
in a specific direction. Some cosmic zones have more
carbon biology (less residue), others have more silicon
or boron biology (more residue). The chemistry of a
zone's template is determined by what survived the
previous Inhale.

**This is a major v5.3 prediction**: the cosmic template
isn't a clean replay — it's a residue-biased re-emergence.
